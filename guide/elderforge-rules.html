ElderForge Rules

This is a placeholder rules page structured for fast editing. Replace sections with your final rules text as you lock things in.

Core Loop

  • Start at the Emberhold.
  • Travel tile-to-tile, resolving encounters (duels, towns, campfires, dungeons, towers, expeditions).
  • Earn cards, Arcstones, and progression benefits as defined by the campaign.

Deck Building

  • No basic lands inside libraries; basics are drawn from external piles instead of drawing a card.
  • Before each duel, roll your six custom color dice and combine the mini-decks for the rolled symbols.
  • Mythic Cards live in Mythic Slots (not in mini-decks) and are always available.

Resources

  • Arcstones: earned primarily from victories and select quests; used for purchases and upgrades.
  • Loss tokens: Stormlash (−1 starting hand size) and Shockmark (−1 starting life) until removed.

Encounters (High Level)

  • Towns: trade / purchase actions (per your Town Tile rules).
  • Campfires: recovery and rare events (e.g., Night of the Veil).
  • Dungeons/Towers/Expeditions: multi-step mini-maps that can award clues and relics.

Tip: if you paste long rules here, use short headings and lots of whitespace. Nobody reads walls of text — not even wizards.
