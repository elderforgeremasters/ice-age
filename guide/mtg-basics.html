MTG Basics

A quick refresher for players who haven’t touched cardboard sorcery in a while.

Turn Structure

  1. Untap
  2. Upkeep
  3. Draw (in ElderForge: you may draw a basic land from the outside pile instead)
  4. Main Phase
  5. Combat
  6. Main Phase
  7. End Step / Cleanup

Spells vs. Abilities

  • Spells are cast from hand and go on the stack.
  • Activated abilities are written “Cost: Effect.”
  • Triggered abilities start with “When/Whenever/At.”

Combat (the shortest useful version)

  • Attackers are declared, then blockers are declared.
  • Damage is dealt (first strike happens earlier if present).
  • Creatures with damage ≥ toughness die (unless something says otherwise).

Common Terms

  • Permanent: a card on the battlefield (creature, land, artifact, enchantment…).
  • Graveyard: discard pile.
  • Library: draw pile.
