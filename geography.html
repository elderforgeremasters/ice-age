This is your campaign atlas: a high-level view of Terisiare in the Ice Age era — where the cold is endless, the roads are worse, and every shortcut has teeth.

Terisiare — Ice Age Era

The Frozen Continent

Use the map below to anchor journeys, dungeons, expeditions, and rumors. It’s meant to feel like something you’d unroll beside a campfire — and then immediately regret because the wind exists.

N

Regions

Kjeldor, Balduvia, Fyndhorn, and the endless ice around Ronom Glacier — the “nearby” places that somehow still require heroic logistics.

Landmarks

Glacial chasms, ruined keeps, frozen marshes, and the kind of coastline that looks calm right up until it eats a ship.

Table use

Pick a route, drop a rumor, or mark a dungeon you’ve cleared. The point is not perfect geography — it’s mood, momentum, and a sense of “we’re not in Kansas, we’re in a snowdrift.”
