How to use this page

Town names, dungeons, and quests pull from real places on this map. If a location sounds like it belongs on a cold, windswept road — it probably does.

Adventure vibe checklist
  • Ronom Glacier — the frozen heart of the endgame.
  • Kjeldor / Krov / Soldev — faction hubs and supply lines.
  • Forests & marshes — where “shortcuts” go to die.
  • Old ruins — the polite word for “trap museum.”
