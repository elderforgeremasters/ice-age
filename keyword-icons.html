Keyword Icons

Full-art cards are beautiful… and occasionally cryptic. This page is a quick “icon → meaning” decoder for the playgroup.

Changeling

This creature is every creature type at all times.

Cycling

Pay the cycling cost, discard this card: draw a card. Great for smoothing draws.

Deathtouch

Any amount of damage this creature deals to a creature is enough to destroy it.

Defender

This creature can’t attack.

Double strike

This creature deals both first-strike and regular combat damage.

Equip

Attach an Equipment to a creature you control. Equipment stays on the battlefield.

Fear

Can’t be blocked except by artifact creatures and/or black creatures.

First strike

This creature deals combat damage before creatures without first strike.

Flash

You may cast this spell any time you could cast an instant.

Flashback

You may cast this card from your graveyard for its flashback cost, then it’s exiled.

Flying

Can’t be blocked except by creatures with flying or reach.

Haste

This creature can attack and use {T} abilities as soon as it enters play.

Hexproof

This permanent can’t be the target of spells or abilities your opponents control (some cards show variant icons).

Horsemanship

Like flying, but only other creatures with horsemanship can block it.

Indestructible

Damage and ‘destroy’ effects don’t destroy this permanent.

Infect

Deals damage to creatures as -1/-1 counters and to players as poison counters.

Intimidate

Can’t be blocked except by artifact creatures and/or creatures that share a color with it.

Kicker

You may pay an extra kicker cost as you cast this spell for a bigger effect.

Landwalk

If the defending player controls the matching land type, this creature can’t be blocked.

Lifelink

Damage this creature deals also causes you to gain that much life.

Menace

Can’t be blocked except by two or more creatures.

Mill

Put cards from the top of a library into its graveyard.

Multikicker

You may pay the multikicker cost any number of times as you cast this spell.

Protection

Prevents damage, enchanting/equipping, blocking, and targeting from the stated quality (often a color).

Reach

Can block creatures with flying.

Scry

Look at the top cards of your library, put any number on bottom and the rest on top.

Shadow

Can block or be blocked only by creatures with shadow.

Shroud

This permanent can’t be the target of spells or abilities (from anyone).

Toxic

When this creature deals combat damage to a player, they get that many poison counters.

Trample

Excess damage to blockers carries over to the defending player/planeswalker.

Vigilance

Attacking doesn’t cause this creature to tap.

Ward

Spells/abilities that target this cost extra; if not paid, they’re countered.

Wither

Deals damage to creatures in the form of -1/-1 counters.
